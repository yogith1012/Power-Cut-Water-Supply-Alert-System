Activity: Objective Definition
Deliverable: Project Object
Project Objectives

1. To develop a web-based system for reporting and monitoring power cuts and water supply interruptions.

2. To provide timely and accurate information about utility service disruptions to users.

3. To allow residents to report power and water supply issues in their locality.

4. To display alerts and updates regarding ongoing and resolved issues.

5. To improve communication between users and utility service providers.

6. To reduce confusion and inconvenience caused by unexpected power and water supply outages.

7. To maintain a centralized database of reported issues and their status.

8. To provide an easy-to-use interface that can be accessed by all users.

9. To enable administrators to verify, update, and manage reported alerts.

10. To help communities plan their daily activities effectively during service interruptions.
