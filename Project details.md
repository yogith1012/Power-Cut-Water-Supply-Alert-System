Project Title

Power Cut & Water Supply Alert System

---

Problem Statement

Many people face difficulties in getting timely information about power cuts and water supply interruptions in their area. Lack of proper communication causes inconvenience, delays in planning daily activities, and creates confusion among residents. Therefore, there is a need for a centralized system that can provide real-time alerts and allow users to report issues related to power and water supply services.

---

Project Objectives

- To provide real-time alerts about power cuts and water supply interruptions.
- To allow users to report issues in their area.
- To improve communication between citizens and service providers.
- To maintain records of reported issues and alerts.
- To create a simple and user-friendly web application.

Project Description

The Power Cut & Water Supply Alert System is a web-based application developed to provide timely notifications regarding power outages and water supply issues. Users can register, log in, and report problems occurring in their locality. The system stores all reports and displays active alerts through a dashboard. This helps residents stay informed and enables quicker response to service interruptions.

Conclusion

The Power Cut & Water Supply Alert System is a useful web application designed to provide timely information about power outages and water supply interruptions. The system enables users to report issues, receive alerts, and track the status of reported problems. By improving communication between citizens and service providers, the application helps reduce inconvenience and ensures better management of public utility services. The project is simple, user-friendly, and can be further enhanced with advanced technologies such as GPS tracking, SMS notifications, and mobile app support in the future.
